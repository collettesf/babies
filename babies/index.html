Sleeping

Sleeping is very important for your baby, to do it the right way

  • A baby should always sleep in a crib without any blankets or objects for safety.

  • A baby should have limited use sleeping in a baby swing to prevent an odd shaped head.

  • A baby should only sleep in a car seat for a short period of time to prevent an odd shaped head.

  • A baby should never sleep in the bed with you, the death rate is higher in bed next to you.

Feeding

Feeding your baby certain ways can have benefits

  • Breastfeeding is the most beneficial way for a healthy baby. They will bond better and the nutrients are specifically designed for your baby.

  • If you can't breastfeed, formula is the next best option.

  • A baby should only have baby cereal when they are 6 months of age or older. Their stomachs can't process it properly.

  • Animal milk should never be given to a baby. There's no good nutrition for the baby.

Hygiene

Make sure to keep up good hygiene for your baby

  • A baby should have a bath once a week at minimal.

  • As soon as the first tooth pops out, brush the babies tooth. Baby cavities can transfer to adult teeth.

  • Wash your babies clothes and linens often to keep the baby clean from germs.

Changing

Are you tired of the sighing, rebel, ungrateful hormonal child that just won't quit. We got you covered.

  • Wipe front to back to prevent infection.

  • Have a clean diaper under a dirty one for easy changing.

  • Change the diaper immediately to prevent a rash, if the bottom is red use rash cream.
